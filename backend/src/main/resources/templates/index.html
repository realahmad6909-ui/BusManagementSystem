Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the UET Transport Service?
Go to the "Student Login" page from the top navigation bar and click on "Register here". Fill in your required details (Name, Registration Number, and Department) and upload your profile picture to create your account.
Why is my account status showing "PENDING"?
For security and verification purposes, all new student accounts are sent directly to the Admin approval queue. The Administrator verifies your credentials and changes your status to APPROVED, after which you can log in to the portal.
How can I track my bus route status and timing?
Once your account is approved by the Admin, simply log in to the Student Portal. Your personal dashboard will display real-time updates regarding your specific bus route, timings, and fee invoices.
Can I change my department or registration number later?
To maintain data integrity, students are not allowed to modify their primary registration details directly. In case of any errors, please contact the Transport Office or the system Administrator to request an update.
